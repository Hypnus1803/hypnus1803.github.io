How It Works

Two simple paths.

The selling page keeps prices visible and short descriptions honest. The give-away page is for anything still useful that I would rather pass on than store.

  • Local pickup is easiest.
  • Short messages are better than long negotiations.
  • The page is updated manually, so availability can change quickly.

Small Rules

Minimal page, direct decisions.

Nothing here is meant to feel like a shop. It is just a calm place to move good objects onward before they sit unused.

If you want something, send a message with the item name and whether you can pick it up soon.

For Selling

Priced items

Selected pieces with a clear price, a short note, and enough detail to decide quickly.

Give Away Free

Useful things, no cost.

Objects that still work or still help, but are better in someone else’s room than in storage.
